DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR Jx VISA

Passport with minimum validity of 6 months

All applicants from eligible country can apply for Jordan visa if they have passport which is valid for minimum 6 months from the date of arrival. Passport must have 2 blank pages.

Image of the passport information page

In some cases Digital passport photo is required. Photo must have plain background and can be taken using phone or any camera.

Although prior booking of hotel is not mandatory however in some case hotel booking information is rquired.

Only in case of business visa, immigration team might ask for business invitation-letter or Buiness card.

It is mandatory have take negative PCR test 72 hours before arrival or Double Vaccination Certificate.

Travelers will be denied entry in case they do not provide either a negtive PCR test or Vaccination Certificate.
